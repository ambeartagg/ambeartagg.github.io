App Log-In Sequence

I designed a login and onboarding sequence for a fictional ink pen subscription app called Ink™, focusing on creating a smooth and intuitive first-time user experience. After conducting initial research and mapping out the user journey, I developed a detailed user-flow diagram to guide design decisions and ensure accessibility and ease of use. I then translated these concepts into an interactive, high-fidelity prototype in Figma, allowing users to navigate the login process and experience the app’s functionality.
