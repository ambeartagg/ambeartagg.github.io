Coffee Shop App Design

This was my first time designing an application. Inspired by app designs from Starbucks, Dutch Bros, and similar companies, I took the opportunity to create my own project. During this experience, I learned to use Adobe XD and explored various design principles. It was exciting to design everything and bring it together into a functioning blueprint.

The Process

To begin my design, I used Adobe Illustrator to create a symbol set. I experimented with the golden spiral to craft unique shapes. By combining these elements, I created symbols that could represent coffee or tea in the app's design.

Creating the Wireframe

After finalizing my symbols, I began piecing together the app wireframe in Adobe XD. I started with a simple wireframe with placeholders for all the elements. Building on this foundation, I added buttons, text, symbols, and other design elements to create each page of the app.

The Final Project

I made the wireframe interactive by linking individual pages. Users can navigate by clicking buttons to transition between pages or dragging along scrollbars, showcasing how the app would function in real-time. This project was both challenging and rewarding, and I am thrilled with the result.
