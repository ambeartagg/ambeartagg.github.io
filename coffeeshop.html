Coffee Shop App Design

This was my first time ever designing an application. I liked the app designs created by Starbucks, Dutch Bros. and similar companies. I took inspiration from these for my own project. In this experience I learned how to use Adobe XD and I learned more about different design principles. It was fun to make everything and put it together into a functioning blueprint.

The Process

To begin my design, I used Adobe Illustrator to create a symbol set. I experimented with the golden spiral to create different shapes. After putting them together, I created some symbols that might be used to represent coffee or tea.

Creating the Wireframe

Once I had my symbols ready, it was time to piece together the app wireframe in Adobe XD. I first started with a much more simple wireframe with placeholders for everything. Once I had that base to start with, I began adding the buttons, text, symbols, and more to each page.

The Final Project

I made my wireframe interactive by connecting each of the individual pages, Clicking on these buttons to transition to a new page or dragging your cursor along a scrollbar demonstrates what navigating the app would look like.
