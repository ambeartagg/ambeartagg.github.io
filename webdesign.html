Mobile Narrative Web Design

I had never really made a website prior to this one, and greatly enjoyed learning to use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I wanted a way to organize some of my music, so I created a large data table with various filters. After sorting through some of my favorite songs, I picked about 100 that I really liked and added them to the table. I also included information about them such as artist, genre, year released, and length.

Exploring My Music Taste

In addition to my data table, I created three graphs. Two of the graphs were created using D3.js, and the third was much simpler. These graphs helped me better understand my music taste, providing data visualizations for the most common era of music I listen to and the most popular genres.
